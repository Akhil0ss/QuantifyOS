# 🧪 Example Tasks for Quantify OS

Quantify OS can handle a wide variety of tasks. Here are some examples of what you can ask the system to do.

## 1. Automation & Productivity
Automate repetitive digital tasks to save time.
- **File Management**: "Organize my Downloads folder into subfolders based on file extensions."
- **Web Scraping**: "Look up the 10 most recent news articles about Bitcoin and save their headlines to news.txt."
- **Content Generation**: "Read my yesterday's research notes and generate a 5-bullet summary for my team."

## 2. Data Analysis
Process and visualize data from various sources.
- **CSV Processing**: "Analyze sales_data.csv and find which product had the highest revenue in October."
- **Trend Detection**: "Compare my website traffic from last month to this month and identify any significant trends."
- **Reporting**: "Generate a weekly summary report based on the logs in the `evolution/` directory."

## 3. Hardware Control
Interface with physical devices and IoT protocols.
- **Device Status**: "Check the status of my MQTT-connected smart lights."
- **IoT Automation**: "If the temperature sensor in the lab exceeds 25°C, send an alert to my workspace logs."
- **Industrial Protocols**: "Read the current telemetry from the Modbus gateway and log it to sensors.json."

## 4. Complex Autonomous Goals
Combine multiple capabilities to achieve a larger objective.
- **Self-Refactoring**: "Scan my custom tools and optimize any that are taking more than 2 seconds to execute."
- **Market Research**: "Identify three competitors in the AI SaaS space and compare their pricing models in a table."
- **Integration**: "Connect my workspace to the Slack API and post a daily status update of my active tasks."
